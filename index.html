Photogrammetry

Turn photos from your phone into high‑quality 3D models that are optimized for AR. What is needed is a series of well-lit photographs taken from many different angles. It analyzes the overlap area between different images to match up landmarks, and then produces a 3D model of the photographed object.

Cross-chain

Blockchain interactions require gas fees which suggests that it can become quite expensive to operate a metaverse platform. A cross-chain is the interoperability between two relatively independent blockchains. In other words, it allows blockchains to speak to one another because they’re built in a standardized way. This interoperability allows the high valume transactions to be handled in low/zero gas fee chain.

Metaverse

In the simplest of nutshells, the metaverse is "a whole new 3D interactive internet". The emergence of the metaverse offers incredible potential for fashion and luxury industry. As fashion immerses itself in the metaverse, it can be separated into two forms: the combination of physical and digital, where clothing can be worn using augmented or virtual realities, and fully digital, where items are sold directly to an avatar.
